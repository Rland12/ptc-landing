Why Paint the City?

We made this app for artists to gain exposure for their work and build inspiring communites that empower each other. There are many times where an artist's credit can be stolen by someone and we hope that by making a digital footprint, an artist can truly own their artwork and those who love to share it will always know who the original creator is.
